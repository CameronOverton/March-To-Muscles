Welcome to March to Muscles!

How It Works

  • Teams & Partners: Every person has been assigned both a team and a partner.
  • Donation Structure: For each workout you complete, your partner will donate $0.50.
  • Workout Requirements: Each workout must be more than 30 minutes long and can be any kind of physical activity.
  • Daily Limit: You are limited to 2 workouts per day.

How to Submit

To submit your workout:

  1. Complete your workout (remember: at least 30 minutes!)
  2. Take a picture of yourself at your workout
  3. Fill out the attached form
  4. Upload your workout photo with the form

Prize & Charity Information

Winning Team Prize: The winning team will take home a gift card worth 25% of total donations!

Charity Contribution: The remaining 75% of donations will go to St. Baldrick's Foundation.

Winner Announcement: April 1st
